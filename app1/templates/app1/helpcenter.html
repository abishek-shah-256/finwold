Help center

The stocks data are live data of NASDAQ stock Exchange of USA.

To predict the stocks ticker listed in NASDAQ. please follow the following instructions.

  • Click on Prediction option on sidebar or Dashboard option on sidebar.

  • Select the company on the search bar and press Enter

  • You will see the 10 days future prediction price of that stocks symbol.
